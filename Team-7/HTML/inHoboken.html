Fun Places to Chill with Friends!

Pier 13:
  • This is a beautiful park with breathtaking views of NYC. It's a large outdoor area with lots of green space, playgrounds, movie nights under the stars, water sports activities, and the best food trucks you can imagine.
  • Pier 13 is one of the best places to go to in the warmer months of the year, so it's definitely a great spot to come to when arriving to campus
Shopping on Washington St:
  • Washington is a very popular street for Stevens students to be. This street is filled with amazing food/drinks places and cute little boutiques.
  • For food and drinks on Washington St there are the common places like Chipotle, McDonald's, Ben & Jerry's, Playa Bowls, Starbucks, and Kung Fu Tea. But the places that are great to try with friends for the first time are Ali Baba, Margherita's, Shaka Bowl, Grimaldi's, and Mamoun's Falafel. There are SO many more amazing places, but these are just a few to start with!
  • For shops on Washington St there are cute stores like Alba Boutique, Noellery, Anthropologie, Athleta, and Dor L'Dor. There are many more shops to check out, but these are just a few to start with and check out with friends!
Carlo's Bakery:
  • Carlo's Bakery is DEFINITELY a spot to check out with friends. Everyone remembers the show Cake Boss from TLC, and it is a must-see! When there, you can check out the famous shop where it all took place and grab some cake and desserts and bring them back to campus for your friends.

Insomnia Cookies:
  • These are by far the BEST cookies you will ever have. They are open till 3am, so maybe while studying for an exam you're craving a snack,so with some friends you head down to Insomnia Cookies and grab a snack.
Hudson River Waterfront:
  • The Hudson River Waterfront is a nice pathway on the western shore of the Hudson River to go on a nice walk or jog with friends. Even if you need a break to be alone once in a while, this is the perfect spot to go to, to get a break from all the stress and unwind.
